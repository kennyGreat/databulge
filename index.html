5. HOW THE DATABULGE SERVICE WORKS

5.1 Selecting a Data Plan

You browse available data plans on the platform, which display the data size (e.g., 500MB, 1GB, 5GB), the applicable network (MTN, Airtel, Glo, T2 and any other displayed on our platform), the validity period, the total cost, and the installment fee (surcharge) applicable to your chosen payment period.

5.2 Installment Payment Structure

Upon subscribing to a plan, you choose a payment period:

  • 7-Day Payment Plan: A 5% surcharge is applied to the market price. Your total cost is divided into daily installments over 7 days.
  • 28-Day Payment Plan: A 10% surcharge is applied to the market price. Your total cost is divided into daily installments over 28 days.

The surcharge represents our service fee for extending the installment facility to you. It is non-refundable once a subscription is created.

5.3 Wallet Funding

Each user maintains a personal wallet ledger on DataBulge. You fund your wallet via bank transfer or any other fund transfer we approve to your dedicated virtual account number. Funds credited to your wallet are:

  • Held in your individual wallet ledger
  • Not pooled with other users’ funds
  • Not invested, lent, or used for any purpose other than purchasing your selected data bundle
  • Automatically applied toward your active subscription the moment they arrive

5.4 Data Delivery

Once your wallet has accumulated the full payment for your selected plan, the system automatically:

  • Purchases the data bundle from the relevant telecommunications vendor
  • Delivers it to the mobile phone number you provided at subscription
  • Sends you an in-app and push notification confirming delivery

Critical Rule: No data bundle, partial data, or any benefit will be delivered or provided until full payment of the selected plan is completed. There is no partial service delivery under any circumstances.

6. REFUND AND AUTO-REFUND POLICY

6.1 Automatic Refund

If a data delivery fails after your full payment is received, the system is designed to automatically refund the deducted amount to your DataBulge wallet within a maximum of seven (7) days of the failure being detected. The following conditions trigger an automatic refund:

  • Both primary and secondary data delivery providers fail to complete the transaction
  • The delivery job remains unprocessed beyond the 2-minute processing window
  • The delivery job is stuck in processing for more than 5 minutes (crash recovery)
  • A delivery confirmation is not received within the allotted processing window

All refunds are credited to your DataBulge wallet and are available for use toward a new subscription immediately. A notification and an inbox message will be sent to you confirming the refund, including the reference number and your updated wallet balance.

6.2 Non-Refundable Amounts

The following are non-refundable under any circumstances:

  • Installment surcharges (service fees) once a subscription has been created
  • Successfully delivered data bundles — once a data bundle has been delivered to your phone number, no refund will be issued regardless of whether the data was used
  • Funds voluntarily transferred to your wallet in error

6.3 Refund to Original Payment Source

DataBulge does not process refunds back to your bank account, debit card, or any external payment instrument. All refunds are credited exclusively to your DataBulge wallet for the purpose of data purchases on our platform.
