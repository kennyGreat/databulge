Terms & Conditions
⚠ Read carefully before creating your account.

1. WHAT DATABULGE IS

DataBulge is a technology platform by Vortexedge Limited that lets you buy mobile data bundles using a structured installment payment system. No data is delivered until full payment is completed.

2. WHAT DATABULGE IS NOT

DataBulge is NOT a financial institution, savings platform, investment vehicle, or thrift collector. Zero return on investment, interest, or profit-sharing of any kind.

3. HOW IT WORKS

Fund your account via bank transfer to your virtual account. Once your balance reaches the full plan cost, data is automatically delivered to your registered phone number.

4. SURCHARGE

5% for 7-day cycles. 10% for 28-day cycles. Non-refundable once subscription is created.

5. REFUND POLICY

If delivery fails, your wallet is automatically refunded within 4 minutes. Successfully delivered data is non-refundable.

6. ELIGIBILITY

  • Must be 18 or older
  • One account per person
  • Valid Nigerian mobile number required

7. GOVERNING LAW

Nigerian law. Contact: support@databulge.com
